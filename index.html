Interesting Facts about chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Pandas spend half the day eating, around 10 to 16 hours
  • Newborn pandas are pink!
  • Panda cubs can climb trees at just the young age of six months!

Interesting Facts about Teddy

  1. Teddy bears got their name from Teddy Roosevelt, who refused to shoot a bear cub, while he was hunting.
  2. The first stuffed teddy bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  3. The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica.
  • Cicadas’ wings are water and bacteria proof.
  • Cicadas can't eat or bite because they lack jaws.

Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. There are two types of camels, one, humped camles, two humped camels.
  3. Camels can drink nearly 200 litres of water in one day.
